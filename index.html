Class One - Welcome to the Sewing Studio

✕

Before Class:

  • Complete the Pre-Quarter Assignment
  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Hand Sewing Needles
    • All-purpose thread in desired color(s)
    • Fabric Scissors
    • Paper Scissors
    • Thread snips/embroidery scissors (optional)

During Class:

  • Practice threading and operation of the industrial lockstitch machine and serger.
  • Begin cutting fabric for sample book.

After Class:

  • Print provided patterns and cut all necessary pattern pieces following the pattern cutting instructions.

Class Two - Getting Started with Fabric Manipulation

✕

Before Class:

    • Box Pleats Pattern
    • Pin Tucks Pattern
    • Tuck and Fold Pattern
  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Juki Industrial Bobbin

    • Hand Sewing Needles

After Class:

Class Seven - Garment Aging and Distressing Techniques

✕

Due:

  • Bring your desired maxi-length dress to class for review
  • Sample Book Productivity
  • Bring the following samples to class for review:
    • Quilting
    • Trapunto
    • Corded trapunto
    • Reverse applique
    • Joined felt shapes

During Class:

  • Students will begin research and sketching for the Riches to Rags project.
  • Begin creating the moodboard, sketches and research for the Riches to Rags project.
  • Use at least 2 of the previously learned fabric manipulation techniques to alter and style a basic maxi dress.
  • Be sure to consider who your character might be in the design and alteration process.
  • Produce a front and back sketch of the altered dress.

After Class:

  • Take copious photos of the original dress in preparation for future submission.
  • Acquire materials for Riches to Rags project.
